Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SpyStaff.

Is SpyStaff legal to use?
Yes, SpyStaff is legal to use in most jurisdictions when used properly. However, it's important to inform your employees about monitoring and follow local laws regarding workplace surveillance. We recommend consulting with legal counsel to ensure compliance with regulations in your region.
How does the pricing work?
SpyStaff offers flexible pricing options. You can choose to pay per minute of active tracking or pay for storage of screenshots and data. Custom pricing is available based on your specific needs and team size. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote.
Can employees see that they are being monitored?
Yes, SpyStaff is designed to be transparent. Employees are notified when monitoring is active, and they can see when screenshots are taken. This transparency helps build trust while still providing the accountability benefits of monitoring.
What kind of reports can I generate?
SpyStaff offers a wide range of reporting options, including productivity reports, time usage breakdowns, project progress tracking, and cost analysis. Reports can be customized to focus on specific teams, projects, or time periods, and can be exported in various formats.
Is my data secure with SpyStaff?
Absolutely. SpyStaff uses enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices for security and regularly undergo security audits. Your data is stored in secure, redundant data centers with strict access controls.
